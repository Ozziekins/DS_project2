Ndukong Godlove Nfor (born 24 October 1983, in Douala), known by his stage name Jovi, 
is a Cameroonian rapper, songwriter, sound engineer, entrepreneur, and record producer 
(under his producer pseudonym Le Monstre).[1] Jovi graduated from the University of Yaoundé II (Soa), 
with a BA Hons in Economics and Business Management.
